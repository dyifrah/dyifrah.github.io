Turkey is a fantastic country to visit. With sites as diverse as historical sites of Istanbul to the seaside resorts on the Aegean and Mediterranean seas, there is something for everyone to enjoy. The Hagia Sofia, first constructed in 537, and the famous Blue Mosque (pictured), are two must-sees for any visitor to the super-city of Istanbul. The ornate mosaics are sure to amaze.

Also highly suggested, is a ferry ride along the Bosphorous Strait, the dividing line between the continents of Europe and Asia. Known for its nightlife, there are many historic taverns where you can enjoy a beer, as well as the classic Turkish anise-flavored alcoholic drink, Raki (think ouzo, only more gross).
